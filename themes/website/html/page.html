OAB Treatment tracker

The OAB treatment tracker allows patients to keep a record of their bladder habits so their doctor can assess how well the treatment is working for them. It has built in prompts reminding the patient to arrange follow up visits with their HCP at the 4 week, 8 week and 12 week mark.
